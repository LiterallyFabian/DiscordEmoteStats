How to get your emote data from Discord

Note that this only works on desktop, as Discord does not allow users to get this data from the mobile app.
  1. Open the DevTools

    You can open the DevTools tab by pressing Ctrl+Shift+I. Then navigate to the Application tab. You might have to press the arrows to see it.


  2. Open the local storage

    Unfold the "Local Storage" tab and open https://discord.com


  3. Copy your emote data

    Find the "EmojiStore" key and copy its value, then paste it on the main page.
